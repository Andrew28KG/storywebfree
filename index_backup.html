Our Story

The Beginning

Dan's Karaoke

We met at a Roblox game called Dan's Karaoke. You invited me to play Roblox and we had our first game there.

It's amazing how something as simple as a virtual karaoke room could be the start of such a meaningful connection.

Gaming Together

Roblox and Mobile Legends

We played more and more. Talking and having fun in Roblox and Mobile Legends. We used to play parkour chained together.

Those gaming sessions weren't just about winning or losing - they were about the conversations we had, the inside jokes we created, and the bond that was forming between us.

Discord Days

Witchies and Chika

You downloaded Discord and we started having our "witchies" and "chika" moments there. Remember our first movie? 'Fractured', I thought it was a very interesting movie and I wanted you to watch it!

Nicknames

Drawing Cats Together

Remember when we played Dan's Karaoke to sing again but instead played hide and seek? That was some really funny and enjoyable moments.
